Our Creative Divisions

Our cotton spinning mill's creative divisions drive innovation, excellence, and efficiency, ensuring exceptional yarn production

  • Raw Material Procurement

    Responsible for sourcing and acquiring high-quality cotton fibers or other raw materials needed for yarn production.

  • Blow Room

    Initial processing stage where raw cotton is cleaned and opened to prepare it for further processing.

  • Carding

    Involves aligning and straightening cotton fibers to create a continuous web, essential for subsequent spinning processes.

  • Spinning

    The core division where fibers are twisted together to form yarn, using spinning frames or machines.
